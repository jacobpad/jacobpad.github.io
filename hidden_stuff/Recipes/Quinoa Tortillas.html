Quinoa Tortillas

Cook Time: 5 mins Servings: Serves 6 Source: pepperplate.com

Ingredients

1 cup quinoa, soaked overnight in water*

3 Tbs. coconut oil, softened

1/2 teaspoon sea salt

Directions

Cover the quinoa with at least an inch of water and soak overnight.

Rinse and immediately add to a Vitamix or high powered blender (if you let the quinoa drain for too long then you'll have to add more liquid.)

Add the coconut oil and salt and blend until the batter is a toothpaste like consistency.

To a silpat or parchment paper lined baking sheet, add about 1/4 cup of the batter and smooth into a 1/4" thick circle. Expect to fit 2-3 tortillas on each baking sheet.

Bake for 5 minutes at 400Â° F.

Remove from oven, let cool briefly and serve.

You may also stack the tortillas between parchment or waxed paper, refrigerate and serve at a later time.
