Mandarin Orange Salad

Source: pepperplate.com

Ingredients

Salad

16 oz cole slaw

4 celery ribs

4 green onions

toasted sesame seeds

toasted sliced almonds

2 cups cooked chicken, cut or shredded

10 oz can of mandarin oranges

1 pkg ramen noodles, crushed

Dressing

1/2 cup oil

3 Tbsp vinegar

2 Tbsp sugar

1 tsp salt

1/2 tsp pepper

1 pkg seasoning from top ramen pkg

Directions

Mix salad

Mix dressing

Enjoy!
